Chicken Tikka Macaroni Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Bake Parlor Tikka Macaroni Packet x1
  2. Chicken
  3. Oil
  4. Salt
  5. Water
  6. Ginger and Garlic paste
  7. Tomatoes
  8. Green Chillies
  9. Ketchup
  10. Black pepper

How to cook?

  • Boil Macaroni:

    • Boil water for macaroni
    • Add salt and one table spoon oil in boiled water and then add macaroni in it to get boiled.
    • When Macaroni is boiled, remove the water.

  • Preapre the Paste:

    • Pour 2 tablespoon oil and add chicken in it.
    • Add Ginger and Garlic paste.
    • Add one tomatoe along with some water.
    • Add tastemaker as well.
    • Let the tomatoes boil and leave it for 5 minutes.
    • Add katchup and greenchillies in the paste when it is about to done.

Add the boiled Macaroni in the prepared paste and mix it very well. Put some black pepper over it in the end and it is ready to eat. Tada!
